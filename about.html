About Us

The Board of Prefects has been part of the college since its establishment in 1989. The earliest recorded Master and Mistress In – Charge of the Board of Prefects were Mr.P. Dhananjyana and Mrs.Wasantha. Since its establishment over 20 Boards have passed over.

Colombo South International College is led by a dedicated team of educators and administrators who are committed to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing learning environment. At the helm of the institution is Mr. Mangala Wijeyakularatne, the Director, who sets the vision and direction for the college.

Assisting him is Mr. Nisal Fernando, the Administrator, who efficiently manages the college's day-to-day operations. The academic leadership is in the capable hands of Mr. B.A. Abeyratne, the Principal, and Mrs. Ranmalee Wettasinghe, the Acting Deputy Principal. They work tirelessly to ensure that students receive the best education possible.

Mr. Oshadha Lakmal and Mrs. Sujatha Vytheeswaran serve as the Master in Charge and Mistress in Charge, respectively, overseeing extracurricular activities and student well-being.

The Staff Committee plays an integral role in the college's functioning, with members like Mr. Gunarathna De Silva, Mr. Sanath Samarasinghe, Mr. M.I. Mazaahir, Mrs. Lalangani Bhareti, Mrs. Yaruni Bulegoda, Mr. Kalpa Amunugama, Mrs. Nirosha Dharmadasa, Mrs. Ashwini Withanage, Mrs. Menaka Attanayake, Mrs. Heshika Alahakoon, and Mrs. Uduli Dharmawardhana contributing their expertise.

Heading the student body are the Head Prefects, Ahamed Yusuf (Boy) and Kadhieja Shafi (Girl), who demonstrate exemplary leadership skills. Assisting them as Deputy Head Prefects are Raaidh Sabry (Boy) and Venuvi De Silva (Girl), who play pivotal roles in maintaining discipline and a positive atmosphere within the school.

Colombo South International College is a place where dedicated educators and responsible student leaders work together to create a dynamic and enriching educational experience for all its students.
