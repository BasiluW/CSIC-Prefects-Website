Our Mission 

The mission of the Colombo South International College's Prefect Guild is to uphold the highest standards of leadership, integrity, and service within our school community.Committed to fostering a positive and inclusive learning environment, the dedicated members of the board work tirelessly to inspire their peers, assist educators, and promote unity among students. Through their leadership and example, they strive to encourage academic excellence, responsible behavior, and a strong sense of belonging, ensuring that every student feels valued and supported throughout their educational journey at our esteemed institution.
