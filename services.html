Projects Done By The Board Of Prefects   





Stationary Donation Camp 

Write for Bright Future was a project to aid students at Kongaspitiya Kanishta Vidyalaya in the Monaragala District to help them gain their rightful education.The Board organised an open-for-all charity which collected money as well as stationeries with monetary value, which were then donated to the respective school in the Uva Province. The stationeries were personally handed out by the prefects who visited the school to deliver the goods.



Car Wash 

The Board of Prefects organized a dynamic car wash fundraiser, transforming their school parking lot into a hub of suds, smiles, and community support. Armed with buckets, sponges, and eco-friendly soaps, the dedicated prefects enthusiastically cleaned and polished vehicles to perfection.



Blood Donation Camp 

The Blood Donation camp held on the 10th of August 2023 was the second ever Blood Donation Camp of CSI College. Approximately 100 donors,including the school members as well as the general public participated.



Triple Threat BasketBall 3x3 

The 3x3 Basketball Tournament was held on the 2nd of July 2023 on the school premises. The aim of this tournament was to help raise funds for the Prefect Board.It was successfully concluded with 22 teams participating in the tournament.



Vesak Dansala 

The Vesak Dansala was a charity event that took place on the 5th of May 2023.With contributions from the Prefect Board, parents and the school administration, the joy of Vesak was shared with the public.



Leadership Camp 

The first ever YOUTH TRANSFORMATIVE LEADERSHIP PROGRAMME of CSI College took place on the 22nd of December 2022 under the guidance of Mr. Suranga Tennakoon.The success of the programme exceeded our expectations with more than 100 participants, including invited schools. This programme aimed to cultivate leadership qualities in the youngsters went beyond as it provided an opportunity for students from different institutes to build new friendships.



First Aid Training Unit 

The first aid training camp for the recruitment of the St. John Ambulance Unit of CSI College was a 2-day programme.This proved to be a necessary unit for the future of the school.Almost 35 candidates successfully completed their training and were qualified.



Past Prefect Association 

The inaugural of the Past Prefect Association (PPA) took place on the 4th of December 2022.The committee members were elected which indicated the successful launch of yet another active society in CSI College.A treasure hunt game was organised as an ice breaker so that the alumni can become comfortable with each other.
